About NM Attendify

NM Attendify is a smart, privacy-first attendance calculator explicitly built for students of SVKM institutions (NM College, Mithibai, UPG, NMIMS). It automates the tedious task of manually calculating how many lectures you can safely miss, turning complex PDF data into a clear, interactive dashboard within seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF data safe?

Absolutely. The PDF parsing happens entirely offline on your device using JavaScript. No files, personal details, or attendance records are ever uploaded to any server or database.

Which colleges are supported?

Any SVKM institution that uses the standard SAP NetWeaver portal format for detailed attendance reports. This typically includes NM College, Mithibai, UPG, and NMIMS courses.

How do I factor in Official Leaves (AG)?

Once you upload your PDF and view your dashboard, simply use the + and - buttons beneath each subject to manually log your approved AGs. The metrics and forecasts will recalculate in real-time.

How is the "Can Miss" value calculated?

It mathematically determines exactly how many consecutive upcoming classes you can safely skip without your percentage dropping strictly below the target percentage you set on the dashboard.
